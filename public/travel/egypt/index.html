Top 5 Egyptian Destinations

Cairo & Giza

Capital city with the Great Pyramids, Sphinx, and Egyptian Museum treasures.

Must-see:
  • Great Pyramid of Giza
  • Sphinx
  • Egyptian Museum

Luxor

Ancient Thebes with Valley of the Kings and magnificent temples.

Must-see:
  • Valley of the Kings
  • Karnak Temple
  • Luxor Temple

Aswan

Southern city with Nubian culture, felucca boats, and Abu Simbel temples.

Must-see:
  • Abu Simbel
  • Philae Temple
  • Nubian Villages

Alexandria

Mediterranean coastal city with ancient library ruins and Greco-Roman heritage.

Must-see:
  • Bibliotheca Alexandrina
  • Qaitbay Citadel
  • Catacombs

Red Sea

World-class diving and snorkeling with coral reefs and beach resorts.

Must-see:
  • Hurghada
  • Sharm El Sheikh
  • Coral Reefs
