Top 5 Destinations

Sydney

Iconic harbor city with Opera House, Harbour Bridge, and beautiful beaches.

Must-see:
  • Sydney Opera House
  • Harbour Bridge
  • Bondi Beach

Melbourne

Cultural capital with street art, coffee culture, and diverse neighborhoods.

Must-see:
  • Federation Square
  • Royal Botanic Gardens
  • Laneways

Great Barrier Reef

World's largest coral reef system, perfect for snorkeling and diving.

Must-see:
  • Coral gardens
  • Marine life
  • Whitsunday Islands

Uluru

Sacred monolith in the heart of Australia, spiritual center of Aboriginal culture.

Must-see:
  • Sunrise/sunset viewing
  • Kata Tjuta
  • Cultural center

Tasmania

Island state with pristine wilderness, unique wildlife, and MONA museum.

Must-see:
  • Cradle Mountain
  • MONA
  • Salamanca Market
