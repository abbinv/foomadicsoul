Top 5 Italian Destinations

Rome

Eternal City with ancient Roman ruins, Vatican City, and Renaissance masterpieces.

Must-see:
  • Colosseum
  • Vatican Museums
  • Trevi Fountain

Florence

Renaissance capital with world-class art museums and stunning architecture.

Must-see:
  • Uffizi Gallery
  • Duomo Cathedral
  • Ponte Vecchio

Venice

Floating city with canals, gondolas, and unique Byzantine architecture.

Must-see:
  • St. Mark's Square
  • Grand Canal
  • Doge's Palace

Amalfi Coast

Dramatic coastline with colorful cliffside towns and Mediterranean beauty.

Must-see:
  • Positano
  • Amalfi Town
  • Ravello Gardens

Tuscany

Rolling hills with vineyards, medieval towns, and world-renowned wine regions.

Must-see:
  • Siena
  • Chianti Wine Region
  • San Gimignano
