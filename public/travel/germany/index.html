Top 5 Destinations

Berlin

Historic capital with rich history, vibrant culture, and modern attractions.

Must-see:
  • Brandenburg Gate
  • Berlin Wall
  • Museum Island

Munich

Bavarian capital famous for Oktoberfest, beer gardens, and Alpine charm.

Must-see:
  • Marienplatz
  • Neuschwanstein Castle
  • English Garden

Cologne

Ancient city on the Rhine with stunning Gothic cathedral and art museums.

Must-see:
  • Cologne Cathedral
  • Rhine River
  • Museum Ludwig

Hamburg

Maritime city with historic port, canals, and vibrant nightlife district.

Must-see:
  • Speicherstadt
  • Reeperbahn
  • Miniatur Wunderland

Dresden

Baroque jewel rebuilt after WWII, known as Florence on the Elbe.

Must-see:
  • Frauenkirche
  • Zwinger Palace
  • Semperoper
