Top 5 Czech Destinations

Prague

Golden city with medieval architecture, Charles Bridge, and Prague Castle.

Must-see:
  • Prague Castle
  • Charles Bridge
  • Old Town Square

Cesky Krumlov

UNESCO medieval town with castle and winding Vltava River.

Must-see:
  • Cesky Krumlov Castle
  • Historic Center
  • Vltava River

Brno

Second largest city with modernist architecture and vibrant cultural scene.

Must-see:
  • Spilberk Castle
  • Villa Tugendhat
  • Cathedral of St. Peter

Karlovy Vary

Famous spa town with hot springs and elegant 19th-century architecture.

Must-see:
  • Hot Springs
  • Mill Colonnade
  • Diana Lookout Tower

Kutna Hora

Historic silver mining town with Gothic cathedral and bone church.

Must-see:
  • Sedlec Ossuary
  • St. Barbara's Cathedral
  • Italian Court
