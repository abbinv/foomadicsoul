Top 5 Thai Destinations

Bangkok

Vibrant capital with golden temples, floating markets, and bustling street life.

Must-see:
  • Grand Palace
  • Wat Pho Temple
  • Chatuchak Market

Phuket

Tropical island paradise with stunning beaches and vibrant nightlife.

Must-see:
  • Patong Beach
  • Phi Phi Islands
  • Big Buddha

Chiang Mai

Northern cultural hub with ancient temples and mountain landscapes.

Must-see:
  • Doi Suthep Temple
  • Old City Temples
  • Night Bazaar

Koh Samui

Palm-fringed island with luxury resorts and pristine beaches.

Must-see:
  • Chaweng Beach
  • Big Buddha Temple
  • Ang Thong Marine Park

Ayutthaya

Ancient capital with UNESCO World Heritage temple ruins.

Must-see:
  • Wat Mahathat
  • Wat Phra Si Sanphet
  • Bang Pa-In Palace
