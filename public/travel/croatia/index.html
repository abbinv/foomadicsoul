Top 5 Croatian Destinations

Dubrovnik

Pearl of the Adriatic with medieval walls and Game of Thrones filming locations.

Must-see:
  • Old Town Walls
  • Stradun Street
  • Lokrum Island

Split

Coastal city built around Roman Emperor Diocletian's palace.

Must-see:
  • Diocletian's Palace
  • Riva Waterfront
  • Marjan Hill

Plitvice Lakes

National park with cascading lakes and waterfalls, UNESCO World Heritage site.

Must-see:
  • 16 Terraced Lakes
  • Veliki Slap Waterfall
  • Wooden Walkways

Hvar

Lavender island with luxury resorts, nightlife, and pristine beaches.

Must-see:
  • Hvar Town
  • Lavender Fields
  • Pakleni Islands

Zagreb

Capital city with Austro-Hungarian architecture and vibrant cultural scene.

Must-see:
  • Upper Town
  • St. Mark's Church
  • Dolac Market
