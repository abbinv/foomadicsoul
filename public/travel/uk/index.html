Top 5 Destinations

London

Historic capital with royal palaces, world-class museums, and iconic landmarks.

Must-see:

  • Tower Bridge
  • British Museum

Edinburgh

Scottish capital with medieval Old Town and elegant Georgian New Town.

Must-see:
  • Edinburgh Castle
  • Royal Mile
  • Arthur's Seat

Bath

Georgian spa town famous for Roman baths and stunning architecture.

Must-see:
  • Roman Baths
  • Royal Crescent
  • Bath Abbey

Mysterious prehistoric monument on Salisbury Plain, UNESCO World Heritage site.

Must-see:
  • Stone Circle
  • Visitor Centre
  • Salisbury Cathedral

Lake District

Scenic national park with lakes, mountains, and literary heritage.

Must-see:
  • Lake Windermere
  • Scafell Pike
  • Beatrix Potter Gallery
