Top 5 Destinations

Beijing

Capital city with ancient palaces, temples, and the iconic Great Wall nearby.

Must-see:
  • Forbidden City
  • Great Wall
  • Temple of Heaven

Shanghai

Modern metropolis with futuristic skyline and historic waterfront districts.

Must-see:
  • The Bund
  • Yu Garden
  • Oriental Pearl Tower

Xi'an

Ancient capital famous for the Terracotta Army and Silk Road history.

Must-see:
  • Terracotta Warriors
  • Ancient City Wall
  • Big Wild Goose Pagoda

Guilin

Stunning karst landscape with limestone peaks and scenic Li River cruises.

Must-see:
  • Li River
  • Reed Flute Cave
  • Elephant Trunk Hill

Chengdu

Panda capital with spicy Sichuan cuisine and relaxed teahouse culture.

Must-see:
  • Giant Panda Base
  • Jinli Ancient Street
  • Wuhou Shrine
