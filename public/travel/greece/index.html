Top 5 Destinations

Volcanic island with white-washed buildings, blue domes, and stunning sunsets.

Must-see:
  • Oia sunset

Mykonos

Cosmopolitan island with vibrant nightlife, beaches, and windmills.

Must-see:
  • Little Venice

Largest Greek island with Minoan palaces, beaches, and mountain villages.

Must-see:
  • Knossos Palace
  • Samaria Gorge
  • Chania Old Town

Delphi

Ancient sanctuary and oracle site with temple ruins and mountain views.

Must-see:
  • Temple of Apollo
  • Ancient Theatre
  • Delphi Museum
