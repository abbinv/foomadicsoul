Top 5 Korean Destinations

Seoul

Dynamic capital with K-pop culture, palaces, and modern technology districts.

Must-see:
  • Gyeongbokgung Palace
  • Myeongdong
  • Gangnam District

Busan

Coastal city with beaches, temples, and colorful Gamcheon Culture Village.

Must-see:
  • Haeundae Beach
  • Gamcheon Village
  • Jagalchi Fish Market

Jeju Island

Volcanic island with natural wonders, beaches, and unique women divers.

Must-see:
  • Hallasan Mountain
  • Seongsan Ilchulbong
  • Manjanggul Cave

Gyeongju

Ancient capital with UNESCO sites and Silla Dynasty historical treasures.

Must-see:
  • Bulguksa Temple
  • Seokguram Grotto
  • Tumuli Park

Jeonju

Traditional hanok village and birthplace of bibimbap, Korea's food capital.

Must-see:
  • Jeonju Hanok Village
  • Gyeonggijeon Shrine
  • Traditional Food Street
