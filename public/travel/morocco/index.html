Top 5 Moroccan Destinations

Marrakech

Red city with bustling souks, palaces, and the famous Jemaa el-Fnaa square.

Must-see:
  • Jemaa el-Fnaa
  • Bahia Palace
  • Majorelle Garden

Fez

Imperial city with the world's largest car-free urban area and ancient medina.

Must-see:
  • Fez el-Bali
  • Al-Qarawiyyin University
  • Tanneries

Casablanca

Modern economic capital with Art Deco architecture and Hassan II Mosque.

Must-see:
  • Hassan II Mosque
  • Corniche
  • Old Medina

Chefchaouen

Blue city in the Rif Mountains with painted blue buildings and mountain views.

Must-see:
  • Blue Streets
  • Kasbah Museum
  • Ras El Maa Waterfall

Sahara Desert

Vast desert with golden dunes, camel treks, and starry night skies.

Must-see:
  • Erg Chebbi Dunes
  • Camel Trekking
  • Desert Camps
