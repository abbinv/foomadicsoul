Top 5 Indian Destinations

Delhi

Capital city blending Mughal heritage with modern India, gateway to Golden Triangle.

Must-see:
  • Red Fort
  • India Gate
  • Lotus Temple

Agra

Home to the iconic Taj Mahal, one of the Seven Wonders of the World.

Must-see:
  • Taj Mahal
  • Agra Fort
  • Mehtab Bagh

Rajasthan

Land of kings with palaces, forts, and desert landscapes in Jaipur and Udaipur.

Must-see:
  • Amber Fort
  • City Palace Udaipur
  • Thar Desert

Kerala

God's Own Country with backwaters, spice plantations, and Ayurvedic treatments.

Must-see:
  • Alleppey Backwaters
  • Munnar Tea Gardens
  • Kochi Fort

Goa

Coastal paradise with Portuguese heritage, beaches, and vibrant nightlife.

Must-see:
  • Baga Beach
  • Old Goa Churches
  • Spice Plantations
