Top 5 Polish Destinations

Krakow

Medieval city with stunning Old Town, Wawel Castle, and rich cultural heritage.

Must-see:
  • Main Market Square
  • Wawel Castle
  • Jewish Quarter

Warsaw

Capital city rebuilt after WWII with modern skyline and historic Old Town.

Must-see:
  • Old Town Square
  • Royal Castle
  • Palace of Culture

Gdansk

Baltic port city with colorful architecture and maritime history.

Must-see:
  • Long Market
  • St. Mary's Church
  • Solidarity Museum

Zakopane

Mountain resort town in the Tatras, perfect for hiking and skiing.

Must-see:
  • Tatra Mountains
  • Krupowki Street
  • Morskie Oko Lake

Wroclaw

University city with Gothic architecture and charming market square.

Must-see:
  • Market Square
  • Cathedral Island
  • Centennial Hall
