Top 5 Traditional Egyptian Dishes

Koshari

Egypt's national dish with rice, lentils, pasta, and spicy tomato sauce.

Key ingredients:
  • Rice & lentils
  • Pasta
  • Tomato sauce

Ful Medames

Traditional breakfast of slow-cooked fava beans with olive oil and spices.

Key ingredients:
  • Fava beans
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic

Molokhia

Green soup made from jute leaves, served with rice and meat.

Key ingredients:
  • Jute leaves
  • Chicken broth
  • Garlic

Mahshi

Stuffed vegetables with rice, herbs, and sometimes meat.

Key ingredients:
  • Vegetables
  • Rice stuffing
  • Fresh herbs

Umm Ali

Egyptian bread pudding dessert with milk, nuts, and raisins.

Key ingredients:
  • Puff pastry
  • Milk
  • Nuts & raisins
