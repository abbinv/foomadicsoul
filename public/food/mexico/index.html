Top 5 Popular Dishes

Tacos al Pastor

Marinated pork cooked on a vertical spit, served with pineapple and onions.

Key ingredients:
  • Pork shoulder
  • Pineapple
  • Corn tortillas

Mole Poblano

Complex sauce with chocolate and chilies, traditionally served over turkey or chicken.

Key ingredients:
  • Various chilies
  • Chocolate
  • Spices and nuts

Chiles Rellenos

Poblano peppers stuffed with cheese, battered, and fried until golden.

Key ingredients:
  • Poblano peppers
  • Cheese
  • Egg batter

Pozole

Traditional soup with hominy corn, pork or chicken, and red chili broth.

Key ingredients:
  • Hominy corn
  • Pork shoulder
  • Guajillo chilies

Churros

Fried dough pastry rolled in cinnamon sugar, often served with chocolate sauce.

Key ingredients:
  • Choux pastry
  • Cinnamon sugar
  • Chocolate sauce
