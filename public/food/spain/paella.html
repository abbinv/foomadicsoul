üë®üç≥ Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in paella pan over medium-high heat

  3. Add green beans and lima beans, cook 5 minutes

  5. Add rice, stir to coat with sofrito
  6. Pour hot stock, bring to boil
  7. Simmer 20 minutes without stirring
  8. Rest 5 minutes before serving
