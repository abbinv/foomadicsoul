Top 5 Traditional Croatian Dishes

Peka

Traditional dish cooked under a bell-shaped lid with meat and vegetables.

Key ingredients:
  • Lamb or veal
  • Potatoes
  • Vegetables

Cevapcici

Grilled minced meat sausages served with onions and flatbread.

Key ingredients:
  • Ground meat
  • Onions
  • Flatbread

Black Risotto

Adriatic specialty with cuttlefish or squid ink giving it black color.

Key ingredients:
  • Arborio rice
  • Cuttlefish
  • Squid ink

Strukli

Traditional pastry with cottage cheese, served baked or boiled.

Key ingredients:
  • Thin pastry
  • Cottage cheese
  • Eggs

Palacinke

Thin crepes filled with jam, chocolate, or cheese.

Key ingredients:
  • Crepe batter
  • Various fillings
  • Sugar
