Portuguese Culinary Heritage

Portuguese cuisine reflects the country's maritime history and global explorations. Known for fresh seafood, especially codfish (bacalhau), Portuguese cooking combines Mediterranean influences with spices from former colonies. The cuisine emphasizes simple preparation methods that highlight natural flavors, creating hearty and satisfying dishes.
