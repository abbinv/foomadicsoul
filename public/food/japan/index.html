Top 5 Popular Dishes

Sushi

Fresh raw fish over seasoned rice, representing the pinnacle of Japanese culinary art.

Key ingredients:
  • Sushi rice
  • Fresh fish
  • Nori seaweed

Ramen

Hearty noodle soup with rich broth, perfect for any season and region.

Key ingredients:
  • Wheat noodles
  • Miso/Shoyu broth
  • Chashu pork

Tempura

Light, crispy battered and fried seafood and vegetables with delicate flavor.

Key ingredients:
  • Shrimp
  • Vegetables
  • Tempura batter

Yakitori

Grilled chicken skewers with tare sauce, popular street food and izakaya staple.

Key ingredients:
  • Chicken
  • Tare sauce
  • Bamboo skewers

Miso Soup

Traditional soup made from fermented soybean paste, served with most meals.

Key ingredients:
  • Miso paste
  • Tofu
  • Wakame seaweed
