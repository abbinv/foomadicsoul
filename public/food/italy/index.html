Top 5 Popular Dishes

Pizza Margherita

Spaghetti Carbonara

Roman pasta dish with eggs, pecorino cheese, guanciale, and black pepper - no cream!

Key ingredients:
  • Spaghetti
  • Guanciale
  • Pecorino Romano

Risotto alla Milanese

Creamy Northern Italian rice dish flavored with saffron, giving it a distinctive golden color.

Key ingredients:
  • Arborio rice
  • Saffron
  • Parmigiano-Reggiano

Braised veal shanks cooked with vegetables, white wine, and broth, traditionally served with risotto.

Key ingredients:
  • Veal shanks
  • Gremolata
  • White wine

Tiramisu

Coffee-flavored dessert made with ladyfingers, mascarpone cheese, eggs, sugar, and cocoa.

Key ingredients:
  • Ladyfingers
  • Mascarpone
  • Espresso coffee
