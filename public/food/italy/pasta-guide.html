Basic Pasta Dough Recipe

Ingredients: 400g tipo 00 flour, 4 large eggs, 1 tsp salt, 1 tbsp olive oil

Method: Create a well with flour, add eggs and mix gradually. Knead for 10 minutes until smooth. Rest for 30 minutes before rolling.

Pro Tips

  • Always salt your pasta water generously
  • Save pasta water to adjust sauce consistency
  • Never rinse cooked pasta
  • Match pasta shapes with appropriate sauces
