Top 5 Traditional Dutch Dishes

Stroopwafel

Thin waffle cookies filled with caramel syrup, best enjoyed warm over coffee.

Key ingredients:
  • Waffle batter
  • Caramel syrup
  • Butter

Bitterballen

Deep-fried meatballs with creamy ragout filling, popular bar snack.

Key ingredients:
  • Beef ragout
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Mustard

Dutch Cheese

World-famous Gouda and Edam cheeses, aged to perfection.

Key ingredients:
  • Cow's milk
  • Cheese cultures
  • Salt

Erwtensoep

Thick split pea soup with smoked sausage, winter comfort food.

Key ingredients:
  • Split peas
  • Smoked sausage
  • Vegetables

Poffertjes

Small fluffy pancakes served with butter and powdered sugar.

Key ingredients:
  • Buckwheat flour
  • Yeast
  • Powdered sugar
