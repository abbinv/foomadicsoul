Top 5 Traditional Russian Dishes

Borscht

Traditional beet soup served hot or cold with sour cream.

Key ingredients:
  • Beetroot
  • Cabbage
  • Sour cream

Beef Stroganoff

Tender beef strips in creamy mushroom sauce, served with rice or noodles.

Key ingredients:
  • Beef strips
  • Mushrooms
  • Sour cream

Pelmeni

Russian dumplings filled with meat, served with butter and sour cream.

Key ingredients:
  • Dough
  • Ground meat
  • Onions

Blini

Thin pancakes served with various toppings, sweet or savory.

Key ingredients:
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Milk

Olivier Salad

Popular potato salad with vegetables, eggs, and mayonnaise.

Key ingredients:
  • Potatoes
  • Eggs
  • Mayonnaise
