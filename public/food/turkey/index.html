Top 5 Popular Dishes

Kebab

Grilled meat skewers with vegetables, served with rice and bread.

Key ingredients:
  • Lamb or chicken
  • Vegetables
  • Spices

Baklava

Sweet pastry with layers of phyllo, nuts, and honey syrup.

Key ingredients:
  • Phyllo pastry
  • Pistachios
  • Honey syrup

Dolma

Stuffed grape leaves with rice, herbs, and sometimes meat.

Key ingredients:
  • Grape leaves
  • Rice
  • Fresh herbs

Turkish Delight

Soft confection dusted with powdered sugar, flavored with rosewater.

Key ingredients:
  • Sugar
  • Starch
  • Rosewater

Meze Platter

Selection of small dishes including hummus, olives, and cheese.

Key ingredients:
  • Hummus
  • Olives
  • Cheese
