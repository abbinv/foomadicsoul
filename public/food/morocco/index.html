Top 5 Traditional Moroccan Dishes

Tagine

Slow-cooked stew with meat, vegetables, and aromatic spices in a clay pot.

Key ingredients:
  • Lamb or chicken
  • Preserved lemons
  • Moroccan spices

Couscous

Steamed semolina grains served with vegetables and meat, Friday tradition.

Key ingredients:
  • Semolina
  • Seven vegetables
  • Meat or chicken

Pastilla

Sweet and savory pie with pigeon or chicken, almonds, and cinnamon.

Key ingredients:
  • Phyllo pastry
  • Pigeon/chicken
  • Almonds

Harira

Traditional soup with tomatoes, lentils, and chickpeas, popular during Ramadan.

Key ingredients:
  • Tomatoes
  • Lentils
  • Chickpeas

Mint Tea

Sweet green tea with fresh mint, served throughout the day as hospitality.

Key ingredients:
  • Green tea
  • Fresh mint
  • Sugar
