Return to the Moon

The Artemis program aims to establish a sustainable human presence on the Moon, serving as a stepping stone for Mars exploration and advancing scientific research in low gravity environments.

Scientific studies in low gravity, astronomy from the far side, and understanding Moon formation and resources.

Extracting water ice for fuel and life support, rare earth elements, and Helium-3 for future fusion energy.
