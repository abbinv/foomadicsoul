The grunge makeup trend is making a powerful comeback, bringing 90s rebellion and effortless cool back to modern beauty routines.

What is Grunge Makeup?

Grunge makeup embraces imperfection with smudged eyeliner, dark lips, and an intentionally undone aesthetic. It's about looking effortlessly edgy rather than perfectly polished.

Key Elements

Smoky, smudged eyes with dark kohl liner, matte or dark lipstick, minimal foundation for a natural skin texture, and tousled hair that looks like you just rolled out of bed.

Modern Twist

Today's grunge incorporates better skincare underneath, sustainable makeup brands, and adaptable looks that work for both casual and professional settings.

Why It's Trending

In an era of social media perfection, grunge makeup offers authenticity and rebellion against unrealistic beauty standards, appealing to Gen Z's desire for genuine self-expression.
