Digital wellness is about creating a healthy relationship with technology that enhances rather than detracts from our well-being.

Screen Time Awareness

Monitor and set limits on device usage. Use built-in screen time tools to understand your digital habits and make conscious choices about technology consumption.

Digital Detox Practices

Regular breaks from devices, tech-free meals, and designated offline hours help restore balance and improve focus, sleep, and real-world connections.

Mindful Technology Use

Be intentional about when and why you use devices. Turn off non-essential notifications, curate your digital environment, and prioritize meaningful online interactions.
