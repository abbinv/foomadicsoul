5 Key Concepts

Existence Precedes Essence

We exist first, then create our own meaning and purpose through our choices and actions.

Key aspects:
  • No predetermined purpose
  • Self-creation through choices
  • Individual responsibility

Radical Freedom

Humans are fundamentally free to choose their path, even in constrained circumstances.

Key aspects:
  • Freedom to choose attitude
  • Responsibility for choices
  • No excuses for inaction

Authenticity

Living true to oneself rather than conforming to external expectations or social roles.

Key aspects:
  • Self-awareness
  • Honest self-expression
  • Rejection of bad faith

Angst and Anxiety

The natural response to recognizing our freedom and the weight of our choices.

Key aspects:
  • Awareness of mortality
  • Burden of choice
  • Confronting meaninglessness

Creating Meaning

Since life has no inherent meaning, we must create our own through passionate engagement.

Key aspects:
  • Personal values
  • Committed action
  • Embracing absurdity
