The fundamental Stoic principle: focus your energy on what you can control and accept what you cannot.

What You Can Control

Your thoughts, actions, responses, values, and effort. These are entirely within your power to influence.

What You Cannot Control

Other people's actions, external events, the past, the future, and outcomes. Accept these with equanimity.
