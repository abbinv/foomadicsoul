⚖️ The Four Virtues

Published on December 2024 | 5 min read

The four cardinal virtues of Stoicism form the foundation of ethical living: wisdom, courage, justice, and temperance.

Wisdom (Sophia)

The ability to navigate complex situations, make sound decisions, and understand what is truly important in life.

Courage (Andreia)

Not just physical bravery, but moral courage to do what is right even when it's difficult or unpopular.
