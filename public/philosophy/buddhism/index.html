☸️ Buddhism

5 Core Teachings

Four Noble Truths

The foundation of Buddhist teaching about suffering and the path to liberation.

Key aspects:
  • Life contains suffering
  • Suffering has a cause
  • Suffering can end

Eightfold Path

The practical guide to ethical and mental development leading to enlightenment.

Key aspects:
  • Right understanding
  • Right intention
  • Right speech

Mindfulness

Present-moment awareness and conscious attention to thoughts, feelings, and sensations.

Key aspects:
  • Present moment focus
  • Non-judgmental awareness
  • Mental clarity

Compassion

Universal love and kindness toward all beings, central to Buddhist practice.

Key aspects:
  • Loving-kindness
  • Empathy for all beings
  • Selfless service

Impermanence

Understanding that all things are temporary and constantly changing.

Key aspects:
  • Nothing is permanent
  • Acceptance of change
  • Non-attachment
